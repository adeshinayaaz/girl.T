Introducing the National Girls in ict Competition, an inititive that seeks to transform the
ICT landscape in Nigeria by creating a platform for girls in secondary schools to
showcase their skils,creativity, and innovation in various ICT-related domains,

This competition is an initiative of the Federal Ministry of Communication,
Innovation, and Digital Economy to encourage female participation in the ICT
sector and empower them to become leaders in this dynamic field. The aim is to
bridge the gender gap in the industry and create equal opportunities for girls to
participate and excel.

More than just a competition
- Unleash your creativity and
problem-solving skills

Are you a young girl with a passion for
technology and innovation? This is your chance
to showcase your skills, unleash your creativity,
and make a significant impact in the world of
ICT.

By participating, you will:


Develop skills and hone your technical
abilities in coding, programming, web
development, cybersecurity, and data
analytics.


Gain Recognition and receive support from
government agencies, educational
institutions, and industry leaders.


Network and learn by engaging in
workshops, mentorship programs, and
networking events to enhance your learning
experience.

How to participate




  • Register:

    Sign up for the competition through the
    registration portal here

  • Form a Team:

    Build a team and register with the support of your teachers.

  • Solve a Problem:

    Identify a problem in your community and
    develop a tech-based solution.

  • Training Programs:

    Participate in a 5-day training program across
    states to enhance your technical skills.

  • Regional Competitions:

    Showcase your solution in regional
    competitions to qualify for the national stage.

  • Virtual Pitching:

    Present your innovative solution in a virtual
    pitching competition evaluated by qualified
    judges.

  • Recognition and Rewards:

    Receive certificates, prizes, and opportunities
    for further education and development.
